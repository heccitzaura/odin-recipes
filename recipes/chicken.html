2 Dollar Chicken

Description

This is the ideal meal that chicken should be while at the same time it is keeping your wallet safe

Recipe

Chicken

  • 4 Chicken Breasts
  • 2 Cups Buttermilk
  • 2 Tbs Basic Chicken Spice

Dredge

  • 3 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 3.5 Tbs Basic Chicken Spice
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Slaw

  • 1/2 Head Green Cabbage
  • 2 Green Onions Very Thinly Sliced
  • 4 Cloves Garlic Grated
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Zest and Juice of 1 Lemon
  • 1/4 Cup Mayo

Curly Fries

  • 5 Medium Russet Potatoes
  • Oil from Frying Chicken
  • 1 Cup Dredge from above
  • 1 Tb paprika
  • 1.5 Cups Water
  • Salt to taste and Fresh Cracked Black Pepper

Chicken Sauce

  • 1/2 Cup Mayo
  • 1/3 Cup Ketchup
  • 3 Tbs Hot Sauce
  • 2 Cloves Garlic Grated
  • 2 Tbs Worcestershire Sauce
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
