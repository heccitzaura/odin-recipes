h1>Cheese Stuffed Garlic Bread Pepperoni Pizza Bites

Description

This is something I saw on Youtube Shorts that looks good

Recipe

  • 1 Roll of premade Pillsbury flaky layers biscuits
  • 1 package of Pepperoni
  • 1 block of Swiss cheese
  • 1 block of mild cheddar cheese
  • 3 TBSP butter (will need some extra)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 TBSP Fresh basil
  • 1 clove of garlic

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350° Fahrenheit
  2. Remove biscuits from the package and lay out
  3. Cut up pepperoni and cheese
  4. Gently spread the biscuit dough by hand
  5. Once spread, fill the biscuit with all three ingredients
  6. Seal the biscuit into a ball, making sure to properly seal the inside to avoid leaking ingredients during baking
  7. Rub some butter on the baking dish to avoid sticking (use extra)
  8. Crack 1 egg into bowl, whisk, and brush over top of uncooked biscuits
  9. Bake biscuits in oven at 350° for 12-15 minutes (larger biscuits will likely take around 18 minutes)
  10. While the biscuits are baking, chop 1 TBSP of basil and 1 clove of garlic
  11. Melt 3 TBSP of butter in a bowl and combine with basil and garlic
  12. Once biscuits are done, brush the butter mixture over the biscuits as they cool
  13. Enjoy!
